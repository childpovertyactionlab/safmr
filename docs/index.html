Small Area Fair Market Rent Difference from Average Rent

This map is generated using

  • Small Area Fair Market Rent from the Department of Housing and Urban Development for 2022
  • Two-Bedroom average rent for multi-family apartment complexes from CoStar for Q1 2022

CoStar data is aggregated to the zip code level and averaged for all complexes within the boundaries of the City of Dallas. The ‘Rent Difference’ metric calculates the difference between SAFMR and average rent for a zip code.

CoStar tracks properties with five or more units, so data on smaller properties with 1-4 units is not reflected here.
